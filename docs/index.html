1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Crypto Tracker App ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application (the "Service"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about our data practices.

2. Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Watchlist Data: Cryptocurrencies you add to your personal watchlist
  • App Preferences: Settings and customization preferences you configure
  • Search Queries: Cryptocurrency searches you perform within the app

Information Automatically Collected

  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version, unique device identifiers
  • Usage Analytics: App usage patterns, feature interactions, and performance metrics
  • Crash Reports: Technical information when the app encounters errors

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Personal Identity: We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or other personally identifiable information
  • Financial Information: We do not collect banking details, wallet addresses, or transaction information
  • Location Data: We do not access or collect your geographic location

3. How We Use Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide Core Functionality: Display cryptocurrency market data and maintain your watchlist
  • Improve User Experience: Optimize app performance and add new features
  • Technical Support: Diagnose and fix technical issues
  • App Analytics: Understand usage patterns to enhance the service

4. Data Sources and Third-Party Services

CoinGecko API

  • We retrieve cryptocurrency market data from CoinGecko's public API
  • No personal information is shared with CoinGecko
  • Data requests are made anonymously

Local Data Storage

  • Watchlist and preferences are stored locally on your device
  • Data is not transmitted to external servers unless explicitly stated
  • You can clear this data by uninstalling the app

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do NOT sell, trade, or rent your information to third parties. We may share information only in these limited circumstances:

  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or government request
  • Safety and Security: To protect our rights, property, or safety, or that of our users
  • Business Transfer: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets (users will be notified)

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information:

  • Local Storage: Sensitive data is stored locally on your device using Android's secure storage mechanisms
  • Encryption: Data transmission uses industry-standard encryption protocols
  • Access Controls: Limited access to any collected data on our systems

7. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your information:

  • Access: View what data we have collected (primarily local watchlist data)
  • Deletion: Remove data by clearing app data or uninstalling the app
  • Opt-out: Disable analytics collection in app settings (if available)
  • Correction: Modify your watchlist and preferences at any time

8. Children's Privacy

Our Service is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete such information.

9. International Data Transfers

As we use CoinGecko's API service, some data requests may be processed internationally. However, no personal information is included in these requests.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy in the app
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date
  • Providing in-app notifications for significant changes
